# Candidate Vision Contract

The public candidate vision must answer:

- why the role matters now;
- what operating tensions exist;
- what Russell believes;
- how the Mission Window Contract works;
- what decisions and measures become clearer;
- why verified career evidence transfers;
- what the first 120 days look like; and
- which executive questions reveal the operating truth.

The site must present one coherent argument rather than a themed portfolio page. The official company identity answers who the work is for. The Mission Window Synchronizer shows how Russell thinks about the work. Application documents reinforce the same thesis without duplicating page copy.
