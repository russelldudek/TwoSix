# Decision Architecture

The Product Associate Director should make these decisions easier:

- what receives product, engineering, UX, growth, and mission capacity;
- what belongs in shared platform leverage versus product-specific workflows;
- what AI may recommend or execute and what remains a named human decision;
- what evidence earns accelerate, scale, integrate, productize, hold, or stop;
- how acquisition and deployment reality shape sequencing;
- which recurring support burdens should become reusable capability; and
- which field signals change the roadmap.

The Mission Window Contract is the shared decision record. It is not a forced interface, generic maturity score, or centralized backlog.
