# Decision Load Audit

- The primary navigation exposes five distinct destinations and one Resume destination only.
- Hero actions lead to two different next layers: verified evidence and the first 120 days.
- The operating artifact offers three bounded product scenarios with a useful starting state.
- The model does not require visitors to construct an empty configuration.
- The former overlapping reset button remains removed because the Helix scenario restores the baseline directly.
- Print controls are not added where native PDF download already satisfies the visitor need.
- The closing application set groups related documents without gating access.
