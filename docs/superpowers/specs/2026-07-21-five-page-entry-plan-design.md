# Five-Page 120-Day Entry Plan Design

## Objective

Expand the Two Six 120-Day Entry Plan from three printed pages to five printed pages while increasing substantive operating depth and slightly increasing visual breathing room. The revised document must remain evidence-grounded, future-facing, useful to a senior product leader, and free of invented Two Six internal facts or unsupported performance targets.

## Page architecture

1. **Cover** — retain the existing role-specific thesis, independent-candidate identity, and tactical-empathy entry principle.
2. **Days 1-30: Read the mission and map the system** — stakeholder listening map, discovery questions, decision-rights inventory, mission/user-access constraints, product/support/integration signals, PM/UX coaching baseline, and concrete outputs.
3. **Days 31-60: Frame the portfolio contract** — Mission Window Contract design, three bounded portfolio hypotheses, qualitative scorecard baselines, decision-rights clarification, AI-assisted product tradecraft pilots, review cadence, guardrails, and expected outputs.
4. **Days 61-90: Prove the mechanism in live work** — live Mission Window Reviews across agentic orchestration, information operations, and sensor/CBRNE contexts; evidence standards; cross-functional commitments; capacity and platform/product conflict resolution; coaching-by-doing; and proof outputs.
5. **Days 91-120: Institutionalize learning and set the next horizon** — operating cadence, field/support/evaluation learning return, roadmap narrative, product economics and productization decisions, team craft priorities, AI-assisted tradecraft experiments, success evidence, and the next-quarter decision agenda.

## Composition

- Use one substantive phase per content page rather than combining two phases on a single sheet.
- Increase body line-height, section spacing, list spacing, and table-cell padding modestly.
- Use compact continuation headers and consistent page furniture.
- Keep every ordinary page within the page-use contract: no page may leave more than roughly 25% of printable height unused.
- Avoid decorative filler, oversized headings, artificial spacers, duplicated prose, unsupported metrics, and invented internal terminology.

## Evidence and language boundaries

- Preserve verified candidate evidence and the existing Two Six role thesis.
- Describe company-specific operating conditions as discovery targets or hypotheses where they are not public facts.
- Use future-facing language: `I would`, `the first 120 days`, `working hypothesis`, and `baseline to establish`.
- Do not claim access to classified information, current clearance, internal product ownership, internal performance data, or authority not established by the role.
- Preserve the complete live candidate-vision URL and native PDF download.

## Verification

- Source regression must assert exactly five `sheet` sections in `120-day-plan.html`, exactly four numbered phase pages, page numbers `2 / 5` through `5 / 5`, and the presence of the four phase ranges.
- Generated PDF audit must require exactly five pages for the entry-plan PDF.
- Rendered browser audit must continue to enforce no overflow, no footer collision, and no ordinary page with more than roughly 25% unused printable height.
- The final release must regenerate the PDF, verify exact live deployment, and update campaign and portfolio learning records because this is a material document correction.
