# Entry Plan Rationale

A 120-day plan fits the role's portfolio scope and player-coach expectations.

- Days 1-30 read the portfolio, users, support patterns, acquisition environment, and product-team craft before changing mechanisms.
- Days 31-60 install a minimal shared decision contract, clarify decision rights, establish baseline definitions, and test AI-assisted product tradecraft with explicit human review.
- Days 61-90 use live product decisions to prove the review cadence, resolve cross-product boundaries, and coach PM/UX leaders inside the work.
- Days 91-120 institutionalize field-learning return, executive roadmap narrative, scorecard cadence, and next-bet decisions.

The plan avoids fabricated percentage targets and treats internal company-state assumptions as discovery questions.
