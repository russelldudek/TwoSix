Mission Window Review

A two-page portfolio conversation for deciding what to accelerate, integrate, prove, productize, hold, or stop.

WINDOW OPEN

Use: one major roadmap or portfolio bet per review. Owner: product leader. Participants: product, UX, engineering, growth/sales, program/support, mission SME, and contracts/acquisition as relevant.

Disposition

Accelerate / Scale

Mission pull, authority, integration, evidence, and acquisition path are sufficiently aligned. Name capacity, owner, next proof, and review date.

Integrate / Productize

Value is credible, but reusable platform, deployment, support, or acquisition capability is the binding work.

Prove / Hold / Stop

Name the missing evidence or constraint. Do not carry a flattering roadmap item without a decision rule.

Closing questions

  1. What did we learn that changes another product or platform decision?
  2. Which recurring support or integration burden should be made reusable?
  3. What will we deliberately not do before the next review?
  4. Who owns the next evidence, by when, and what decision will it unlock?
