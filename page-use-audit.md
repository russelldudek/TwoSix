# Page-Use Audit

The generated-document review rejects technically correct but visibly poor pagination.

## Required checks

- Resume: exactly two pages.
- Cover letter: exactly one page.
- Interview thesis brief: exactly four pages.
- 120-day entry plan: exactly three pages.
- Mission Window Review: exactly two pages.
- Ordinary pages may not overlap absolute footers.
- Ordinary pages may not leave more than roughly one quarter of the printable height unused.
- Dark covers are reviewed as deliberate cover compositions rather than ordinary body pages.
- Screen sheets must reflow at tablet and mobile widths without horizontal overflow.
- Print geometry remains exact US Letter.
- Native PDF download controls remain visible on screen and hidden in print.

These checks run against the rendered HTML and the generated PDF files before publication.
