# Responsive Composition Record

## Desktop and laptop

The candidate thesis and Mission Window Synchronizer share the opening composition. The artifact retains four horizontal decision gates, a complete disposition, and one-shot motion.

## Tablet

The hero becomes a deliberate vertical sequence: thesis first, operating artifact second. Navigation becomes an accessible disclosure menu so no destination is silently removed.

## 390-pixel mobile

The four decision gates become a two-by-two semantic card composition with horizontal labels, state text, and compact visual indicators. The wide desktop track is removed because it no longer adds causal value at this scale.

## 320-pixel narrow phone

Scenario controls and decision gates become single-column records. Essential state, disposition, and evidence remain visible without internal horizontal scrolling.

## Reduced motion

All scenario content updates immediately and settles without pulse or staggered gate animation.
