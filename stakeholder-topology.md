# Stakeholder Topology

## Executive and portfolio leadership

Chief Product Officer, VP Product Management, product engineering leadership, growth and sales leadership, and business-unit executives.

## Product delivery

Product managers, UX designers, product engineers, data and AI specialists, assurance partners, and platform owners.

## Mission and program work

Mission subject-matter experts, program leaders, operators, customer representatives, integration teams, support teams, and field deployment teams.

## Commercial and acquisition

Contracts, acquisition, pricing, sales, growth, partner, and vehicle owners.

## External constraints

Security classification, data access, legacy integration, doctrine, policy, procurement timing, deployment environment, training, sustainment, reliability, and mission consequence.

The Mission Window Contract gives these stakeholders a shared decision record without replacing their distinct expertise or decision rights.
