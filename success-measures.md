# Success Measure Framework

No unsupported target values are assumed. Baselines and targets should be established with product, engineering, UX, mission, growth, program, acquisition, and support leaders.

## Mission outcome

- time-to-decision;
- action quality;
- mission completion evidence;
- risk avoided or contained.

## Product behavior

- discovery-to-decision latency;
- backlog clarity;
- roadmap confidence;
- unresolved dependency age.

## Trust and risk

- traceability;
- named human checkpoints;
- evaluation coverage;
- reliability;
- fallback readiness.

## Adoption and support

- time-to-first-value;
- training burden;
- recurring support drivers;
- operator review burden.

## Economics and acquisition

- deployment effort;
- reuse;
- support intensity;
- acquisition-vehicle fit;
- sustainment burden.

## Learning speed

- field evidence captured;
- roadmap or platform decisions changed;
- recurring integration and support patterns converted into reusable capability.
