# Visual Benchmark Criteria

The candidate vision is reviewed for:

- immediate Two Six recognition;
- one dominant thesis and one dominant visual idea;
- executive typography, spacing, depth, and restraint;
- causal readability of the Mission Window Synchronizer;
- distinct product states and stable motion settlement;
- complete keyboard and reduced-motion behavior;
- deliberate desktop, tablet, 390-pixel, and 320-pixel composition;
- full-page pacing beyond the hero;
- integrated final document navigation; and
- visual distinction from prior candidate campaigns without suppressing authentic employer identity.

A structural pass cannot overrule a visible defect identified in the rendered experience.
